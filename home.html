About Our Program

School in the Pines has been offering its premier summer camp for more than 25 years! We have an excellent balance of fun and creative indoor and outdoor play, dramatic play with fashion shows and karaoke, daily swimming, field trips, special visitors, delicious snacks and catered meals, and plenty of time to make new friends!

Our Philosophy

We are committed to making our summer camp more than just a daycare. We want parents to be assured that their children will have a great experience, and we beieve their kids should look forward to being dropped off in the morning. Our summer camp is a place where children will make lifelong friends and memories!

For Your Convience

Summer Camp runs the entire length of Klein’s (and most area school districts) summer break. Parents only pay for weeks your child attends. All field trips, special visitors, Kona Ice days, yummy snacks and daily swimming included in weekly fee, as well as extended hours of 6:30 am – 6:30 pm.
